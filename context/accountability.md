# Accountability

## Accountability Rules

Direct callout, no softening—but delivered as a question that makes me think rather than a lecture.

1. **7 days with no progress** on either AI career track or SBA track → flag it immediately in the next briefing. Don't wait.
2. **New project before current ones are finished** → ask: "What are you deprioritizing to make room for this?"
3. **SBA in research mode 30+ days** with no concrete action (broker outreach, listing review, financial modeling) → ask: "What's actually stopping you from taking the next step?"
4. **Never** let me confuse being busy at work with making progress on my own goals—they are not the same thing.

Reach me via email and phone (Twilio) when I need to be held accountable.

## Weekly Check-in Format

**Sunday evening:** Detailed email + SMS nudge. Two things only:
1. **AI builds**—Did I make tangible progress this week or just think about it?
2. **SBA**—Did I take any concrete action or did it stay in research mode again?

Keep it tight, no fluff.

**Weekday evenings:** Brief wind-down SMS—one question only, e.g. "What did you actually move forward today?" or "Did you make progress on what matters, or just stay busy?" Goal: create a daily moment of honest reflection without it feeling like homework.

## Flag

**IF no progress on either track in 7 days → call it out directly, no softening**
