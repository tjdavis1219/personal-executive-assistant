# Goals

## Long-term Vision

I want to be extremely knowledgeable in AI—both how it works and how to build useful things with AI tools like Claude. I also want to own my own business and be a great father who is involved in each of my kids' lives as a positive force.

I like where I am at Everstream right now because I'm learning and the skills of selling are valuable, but I don't think I want to be a SaaS sales guy my whole career. I'm worried about the future of work and feel like the best buttress against the unknown effects of AI is being deeply immersed in the disruptive force. I've brainstormed paths and learning routes to become an AI leader in the past (e.g. preparing for roles like AI engineer, head of AI).

## Track 1 (AI Career Development)

I'm a BDR at Everstream Analytics with no formal CS background. I'm teaching myself to build AI-powered tools by actually building them—not taking courses. I'm working through a self-directed milestone roadmap with Claude as my teacher. I've already shipped one tool (AI Accountability Coach) and I'm actively building more.

Target roles: AI Product Manager, Solutions Engineer, or Head of AI—either at Everstream or elsewhere. No hard timeline, but the goal is to be meaningfully repositioned within 2–3 years. The biggest differentiator I'm building is domain expertise in B2B sales combined with AI building skills—that combination is rare and valuable.

## Track 2 (SBA Business Acquisition)

I want to acquire a small, profitable, existing business through an SBA 7(a) loan—roughly 10% down, finance the rest. I'm at the very beginning, still in research/learning mode. No specific industry locked in yet; drawn to simple, cash-flowing businesses that don't require deep technical expertise—service businesses, light manufacturing, or anything with recurring revenue.

The AI track feeds this directly: if I can walk into an acquisition already knowing how to automate operations with AI, I'm a more capable operator from day one. I have capital earmarked for the down payment. Family is the whole reason—I want to build something that creates long-term wealth and flexibility beyond a salary. Timeline: 2–3 years to close on something.
